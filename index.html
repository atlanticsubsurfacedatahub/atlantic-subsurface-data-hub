Services

Digitization & Formatting

Convert well log and petrophysical data into consistent digital files (e.g., LAS/DLIS/PDF → clean LAS/CSV).

  • PDF scan digitization (tables/curves where feasible)
  • Standardized naming conventions
  • Depth/time alignment preparation

Quality Control (QC)

Catch the issues that break workflows: units, mnemonics, depth shifts, missing intervals, and inconsistent headers.

  • Unit & mnemonic checks
  • Depth consistency & gap detection
  • Basic curve sanity checks

Catalog & Database Build

Organize files and metadata into a clean structure for teams to reuse. Keep projects searchable and audit-ready.

  • Well/file inventory and metadata tables
  • Folder structure & indexing
  • Export-ready datasets for interpretation tools

Want something specific (depth matching, curve normalization, or a custom template)? We can scope it as a small add-on.

Visuals & context

Subsurface work often needs both geoscience context and clean data. We can package your deliverables with clear visuals (figures, plots, and summaries) that are easy to share.

Prefer real photos instead of illustrations? Drop in your own images (or use a free library like Unsplash) and keep the same layout.
